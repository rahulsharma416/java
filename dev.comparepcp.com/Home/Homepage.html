Why ComparePCP?

Buying a car is a tedious task which can take weeks, some times months, before you decide which car to buy. This involves two key bits, first find a car you love and then look for a finance deal that is right for you. Often a combination of these two helps you decide.

ComparePCP brings all the latest PCP deals for different car brands. We make it easier for you to find and compare the various PCP deals on new cars.

ComparePCP is a fair and free service for customers and dealers. We neither charge customers nor dealers.

How to use?

When you have a car in your mind

Step 1 : Go to Compare > "PCP deals by car"
Step 2 : Enter the details
Step 3 : Check monthly payments on your preferred car
Step 4 : Scroll down and check “Other cars in similar monthly payment”. If you don’t find or like other deals, then go to “PCP deals by monthly budget”

PCP Deals by monthly budget

Step 1 : Go to Compare > "PCP deals by monthly budget"
Step 2 : Enter the details (Budget, deposit, term, Mileage, postcode)
Step 3 : Check all the cars in your monthly budget
Step 4 : Filter the cars

How to use?

Compare & Save deals

Step 1 : Compare the deals with all the details next to each other.
Step 2 : Save deals by signing up or Call dealers directly.

Call dealers

Step 1 : Call dealer to discuss the details.
Step 2 : A customer will buy directly from the dealers.
Step 3 : Comparepcp.com neither charges customers nor to the dealers.
